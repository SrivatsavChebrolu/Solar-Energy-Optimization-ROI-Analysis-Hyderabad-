# Solar-Energy-Optimization-ROI-Analysis-Hyderabad-

# ☀️ Solar Energy Optimization & ROI Analysis Platform

A data-driven solar planning tool that models real-world energy generation, consumption, and financial outcomes for residential users in Hyderabad.

---

## 🚀 Overview

This project builds an example **decision-support system** that simulates:

* Monthly solar energy generation using real irradiance data
* Household consumption patterns (including EV usage)
* Energy flow (self-consumption vs export)
* Financial outcomes (savings, export income, ROI, payback period)

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### ⚡ Energy Modeling

* Monthly solar generation using Hyderabad-specific irradiance data
* Seasonal consumption modeling (summer peaks, monsoon dips)
* Dynamic energy matching (used vs excess energy)

### 🏠 System Design

* Dynamic system sizing:

  * Roof-based (maximize usage)
  * Demand-based (match consumption)
  * Budget-based (cost constrained)
* Panel type selection (mono, poly, bifacial)

### 🚗 EV Integration

* Models EV energy consumption (car & bike)
* Shows impact of EV adoption on solar utilization
* Demonstrates how EVs improve ROI by increasing self-consumption

### 💰 Financial Analysis

* Realistic tariff modeling
* Net metering and export pricing
* Maintenance costs included
* Payback period and long-term projection

### 📊 Monthly Analysis

* Month-by-month breakdown of:

  * Generation
  * Consumption
  * Used energy
  * Excess energy
  * Savings

### 💡 Explanation Layer (Key Feature)

Provides insights such as:

* Why ROI is high or low
* Whether the system is over/under-sized
* How to improve efficiency (EV usage, resizing, etc.)

---

## 🧩 Tech Stack

* Python
* Streamlit (UI)
* Matplotlib (visualization)
* Custom data modeling (no external APIs)

---

## 🧪 Example Insights

* Solar systems perform best when generation matches consumption
* Oversizing leads to low-value energy export and longer payback
* EV adoption significantly improves solar utilization
* Seasonal variation impacts both generation and savings

---

## 🌍 Real-World Relevance

This project demonstrates how solar adoption decisions depend on:

* Energy consumption patterns
* System sizing strategy
* Policy constraints (net metering, tariffs)
* Lifestyle choices (EV usage, electrification)
